Our Favourite Recipes This Month

  • Banana Bread

    Our take on the classic banana bread balances all-purpose and whole wheat flours for added fibre without sacrificing flavour. Three ripe bananas and yogurt keep the loaf moist, while cinnamon and optional chocolate chips provide warmth and richness. For an eye-catching finish, crown the batter with a sliced banana before baking until golden.

  • Sheet Pan Hash with Baked Eggs

    Put your skillet away because every ingredient of this recipe is going straight on a baking sheet. This effortless hash brings together wholesome ingredients and baked eggs in one convenient dish. Perfect for feeding a crowd, and a breeze to clean up, this recipe will become a regular star at your table.

Sunday Meal Prep Made Easy

Prep once. Enjoy all week.

  • Ham, Cheese and Spinach Quiche

    Light, fluffy, and filled with hearty ingredients, this quiche is a real crowd-pleaser. Ingredients like Gruyère cheese, smoked ham, and baby spinach are combined with a creamy egg custard and baked until golden brown. Serve it warm or at room temperature, and enjoy the compliments from both family and friends with every slice.

  • Sheet Pan Breakfast Sandwich

    These sheet pan breakfast sandwiches are a time-saving solution for feeding a crowd or prepping meals in advance. Eggs and cheese bake together in one large batch, creating perfectly portioned squares to nestle between English muffin halves. Make the egg sheet ahead and reheat individual portions as needed. Add your favourite veggies to the egg mixture for extra flavour and fibre.

  • Breakfast Burritos

    Start your day on a high note with this breakfast burrito. Featuring fluffy scrambled eggs and your choice of delicious fillings, its guaranteed to be a satisfying morning meal. You can customize the ingredients to suit your tastes for a breakfast treat that hits just right every time.
